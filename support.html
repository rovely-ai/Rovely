What is Rovely?
Rovely is a travel app that helps you build trips your way — use AI, start from scratch, or pull from ready-made itineraries. Customize every detail and see your entire journey come to life on an interactive map.
How do I create a trip?
Open the app, tap Plan Trip, and choose how you want to start — use Rovely AI, build from scratch, or pick an itinerary template. You can edit anything at any time, no matter how you begin. Add custom checklists along the way so you never leave anything behind.
Can I share my trips with friends?
You can download or share your trips externally. In-app sharing and collaborating isn’t available yet, but it’s coming in a future update.
Do I need an account to use Rovely?
Yes, an account lets you build and save your trips, checklists, and preferences across all your devices.
Does Rovely collect my personal data?
We do not collect location data or analytics.
Can I use Rovely offline?
Yes, you can view your trips offline. You’ll need an internet connection for full features like building or updating trips.
How do I delete my account or data?
You can delete your account by going to Profile → Personal Details → Delete Account, or by emailing a deletion request. We process all requests within 30 days. Deleting your account permanently erases all your trip data — we don’t retain it.
Is Rovely free?
Yes, Rovely is free!
Which countries or regions does Rovely support?
Rovely can be used worldwide. Destination suggestions and templates cover major travel hubs globally, and you can manually add any location.
Who can I contact for support?
You can reach us at ai.roam@Rovely.com for any questions, feedback, or troubleshooting.
How do I report a bug or provide feedback?
Use the Feedback option in the app or email us. We welcome all suggestions to improve Rovely.
